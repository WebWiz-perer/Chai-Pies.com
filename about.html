About Us

Welcome to Chai & Pies, where flavors and cultures meet to create an unforgettable experience. Our cafe was born out of a love for both chai, the soul-soothing beverage that has been a symbol of warmth and community for centuries, and pies, those timeless comfort foods that evoke a sense of home. At Chai & Pies, we don’t just serve food; we craft moments where people can unwind, connect, and savor the best of both worlds. We believe that food has the power to bring people together. Our menu is a fusion of flavors inspired by diverse culinary traditions. From our flaky, savory samosas to our freshly baked pies, each bite is a blend of spice, sweetness, and soul. Whether you’re looking for a hearty meal, a sweet treat, or just a quiet moment with a cup of chai, we’ve got something for everyone.

Our Philosophy: Bringing people together with time.
